At its inception-and functioning in parallel with the VirusTotal integration—Exodia Labs operated with a highly specialized profile, primarily focused on the deep-dive analysis of digital ecosystems such as the Google Chrome Web Store and other extension marketplaces.

However, after months of internal research, the profound potential of emerging technologies became undeniably clear, as did the realization of how forcefully these advancements are poised to disrupt the entire cybersecurity landscape.

Free from industry hype and "unicorn" myths, and anchored strictly by pure research, innovation, creativity, and relentless experimentation, the decision was made to extend the laboratory's operational domains far beyond its original boundaries. The vast and complex spectrum of possibilities that unfolded demanded a broader, more ambitious scope.

This strategic pivot is not a mere adjustment—it is an absolute necessity. In a disruptive era where the technological paradigm shifts daily, adaptation is not just important; it is an imperative for survival and continued vanguard research.
